BITS Open Sports Meet, 2015

“You win some, you lose some, and some get rained out, but you gotta suit up for them all.”– J. Askenberg

The 30th edition of BOSM is here with a platform for you and your college to showcase your talents on the field. Brace yourselves for four days worth of floodlit matches, unbridled enthusiasm and heart-felt cheering. With last year’s phenomenal participation by colleges from all over India and abroad, BOSM 2015 promises to be bigger and better than its past incarnations.

Have the grit. Play your guts out. Take home the glory.

18th September - 22nd September.

Keep your head in the game.

Baxter- The Desert Eagle

To pick up your morale when your strength fails you.

For that much needed oomph in this unforgiving desert town.

Every festival needs a fitting mascot. Baxter, the Desert Eagle, aptly embodies the virtues of every sports person - Skill, Perseverance and Team Spirit. Transcending beyond individual sports, Baxter binds all players with the essence of BOSM.

Professional Show - The F16s
The F16s are an alternative/electro-rock band,known for their high energy eccentric live performances .They released their debut EP , Kaleidoscope in 2013 which went on to become a great success. The record acts as an effective acid test, laying the groundwork for an arresting live set. Having competed with some of the most promising upcoming bands in the country they won an all expenses paid trip to Rubber Tracks studios in New York where they recorded their Nobody’s Gonna Wait EP. They are set to etch their name in history by becoming the first band ever to perform live in BOSM.

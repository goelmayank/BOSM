About BOSM

"Courage is not the absence of fear. It is being afraid but being able to control that fear so you are able to perform at your highest ability. That's what makes a champion."

Because champions rise, champions fall, rise again, win it all.

It is not about winning, it is about taking part and becoming your own potential.

The BITS Open Sports Meet, or BOSM, as all know it, is one of the largest student managed Sports fests of the country. The four-day festival encompasses everything about sports.Aimed at providing a platform to colleges from all over India to showcase their talents, promoting healthy competition, it gives attendees an opportunity to take part in a variety of sports and events. With the last year.s edition witnessing phenomenal participation from colleges both from India and abroad, the 29th edition guarantees excitement well supplemented by floodlit matches in the evening and the unbridled enthusiasm of the crowds for highly competitive track and off-track events, in the true spirit of sportsmanship and camaraderie.

18th September - 22nd September. BOSM 2015.

Come, battle it out, with grit and guts, in pursuit of glory.


Baxter, The Mascot

Every festival needs a mascot symbol of the entire event. when it comes to BOSM, look no further than Baxter, the Desert Eagle. Officila mascot of the fest since 2013, Baxter epitomizes the virtues of strength, perseverance, endurance and the spirit of sportsmanship, providing a unique avenue for potential parters to interact with the paticipants and the audience at a more personal level.

YODH

More Than Just A Game..

“Do you know what my favorite part of game is? The opportunity to play“

                                                                                                                     -Mike Singletary

How many kids in India actually get a chance to experience this?

Shockingly, in India , 43.48% of schools don’t have recreational facilities for their kids. Rajasthan fares even worse, with  51.61% of the schools having no playgrounds. This BOSM, Nirmaan-Pilani presents a unique opportunity for all kids to showcase their sporting talent.

YODH 2014 is a two-day inter-school sports event. One of the most brilliant initiatives taken by BITS. It is an effort to give a chance to students to develop their interest in sports, an effort to create some opportunities for talented, deserving students.

This sports extravaganza, with numerous sporting events like athletics, chess, cricket, badminton and many more events,  will witness participation from more than 200 students of classes 6th to 10th.  10+ schools from in and around Pilani will be participating in the event. These schools have only one thing in common: no playground facilities, no dedicated sports teachers, lack of knowledge about the importance of sports in the personality building of a student, yet a burning desire to overcome these difficulties and emerge as winners. Yodh is an enterprise from our side to provide a platform to let the school authorities as well as students discover their hidden potential, which has remained untapped all these years.

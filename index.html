More Than Just A Game..

“Do you know what my favorite part of game is? The opportunity to play“

                                                                                                                     -Mike Singletary

How many kids in India actually get a chance to experience this?

Shockingly, in India , 43.48% of schools don’t have recreational facilities for their kids. Rajasthan fares even worse, with  51.61% of the schools having no playgrounds. This BOSM, Nirmaan-Pilani presents a unique opportunity for all kids to showcase their sporting talent.

YODH 2014 is a two-day inter-school sports event. One of the most brilliant initiatives taken by BITS. It is an effort to give a chance to students to develop their interest in sports, an effort to create some opportunities for talented, deserving students.

This sports extravaganza, with numerous sporting events like athletics, chess, cricket, badminton and many more events,  will witness participation from more than 200 students of classes 6th to 10th.  10+ schools from in and around Pilani will be participating in the event. These schools have only one thing in common: no playground facilities, no dedicated sports teachers, lack of knowledge about the importance of sports in the personality building of a student, yet a burning desire to overcome these difficulties and emerge as winners. Yodh is an enterprise from our side to provide a platform to let the school authorities as well as students discover their hidden potential, which has remained untapped all these years.
